ABOUT US

HOW IT STARTED

*

The vast majority of our menu about 90% are vegan dishes - prepared from the best ingredients, served with attention to every little detail. We are able to extract extremely intense and original flavors from plant products and conjure up dishes that will captivate everyone. Our secret is experience, innovation in the creation of dishes and commitment resulting from culinary passions. The restaurant's menu changes with the coming seasons.

— Our Bar —

we are captivated by the composition of cocktails

Fancy drinks, exquisite alcohol, organic coffee. We offer exotic and classic drinks, which are specially prepared for you by professional bartenders. In our Drink Bar you will find a wide selection of delicious spirits from around the world.

— Our Interiors —

we charm with the atmosphere and delight with the interior

Cinnamone is a remarkable restaurant located in one of the most fashionable places in London. Decorated with taste and attention to every detail, modern, glazed interior with a pleasant climate and a relaxed atmosphere. A perfect place for lunch, meeting friends, family celebrations and business meetings.
